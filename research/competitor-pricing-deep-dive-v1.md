# Slacked.co Competitor Pricing Deep-Dive
## Exhaustive Research — March 2026

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

This report analyzes 15+ competitors across three categories: AI/automation platforms, marketing agencies (DFY model), and website deployment services. The core finding is clear: **Slacked is dramatically underpriced for the value it delivers, and the current offering is too narrow.**

The market breaks into two segments:
1. **Self-serve AI tools** ($20–$300/mo) — clients do the work themselves with AI assistance
2. **Done-For-You agencies** ($3,000–$30,000+/mo) — agencies handle everything

Slacked sits in the DFY category but prices like a self-serve tool. The gap between self-serve tools and full-service agencies is enormous, and that's exactly where Slacked should position: **AI-powered DFY at a fraction of traditional agency cost, with a real client dashboard and Basecamp project management.**
