## Research Insights Applied

1. **$5K/month is the market sweet spot** for multi-practice-area law firm SEO (source: SEOProfy, Fortress Growth)
2. **Reports must tie to business outcomes** — leads generated, cases signed — not vanity metrics (source: Swydo, AgencyAnalytics)
3. **Onboarding has 3 phases:** housekeeping → information gathering → execution (source: AgencyAnalytics, Zapier)
4. **Local SEO for Austin law = GBP optimization + neighborhood-aware content + citation management** (source: AustinSEO.ai, AcuteSEO)
5. **Transparent reporting builds trust** even when results fall short — always show the data (source: Swydo, Funnel.io)
6. **6-12 month minimum commitment** is standard for SEO retainers — SEO takes time (source: SEOProfy)
7. **Client portal visibility** is a 2026 differentiator — Basecamp serves this role for Slacked (source: ManyRequests, Plutio)
