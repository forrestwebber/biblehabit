## Template Structure

### MESSAGE BOARD (Client-Visible)
- **Welcome to Slacked!** — Onboarding roadmap with 3 steps + service overview
- Monthly reports posted here (created monthly by agents)

### TODO LISTS

#### 1. "This Month's Deliverables" (Client-Visible)
Pre-loaded sample items:
- Website SEO audit — Month 1
- Google Business Profile optimization
- 4 blog posts (SEO-optimized)
- Social media content calendar

#### 2. "Onboarding Checklist" (Client-Visible)
Pre-loaded items:
- Share website login credentials (WordPress/CMS)
- Share Google Analytics / Search Console access
- Fill out Brand Guidelines questionnaire
- Share social media account credentials
- Confirm target audience and service areas

#### 3. "Internal Work Queue" (Hidden from Client)
Pre-loaded agent tasks:
- [AGENT] Run initial keyword research (Atlas)
- [AGENT] Competitor backlink analysis (Aria)
- [AGENT] Set up analytics dashboards (Rowan)

#### 4. "Completed Archive" (Client-Visible)
Empty — completed deliverables move here

### DOCS & FILES

#### 1. "Service Agreement" (Client-Visible)
Full service breakdown:
- What they get (content, SEO, social, GBP, analytics)
- Service level (24hr response, monthly call, 2 revision rounds)
- What we need from them
- Timeline (Week 1-4 + Month 2+)

#### 2. "Brand Guidelines" (Hidden Until Filled)
Questionnaire template:
- Brand Identity (name, tagline, mission)
- Visual Identity (colors, fonts, logos)
- Brand Voice (tone, words to use/avoid)
- Target Audience (demographics, pain points, online presence)
- Competitors (top 3 + differentiation)

#### 3. "Strategy Notes (INTERNAL)" (Hidden from Client)
Internal planning doc:
- Client overview (tier, budget, contract start, contact)
- Strategy (KPI, quick wins, long-term plays, content pillars)
- Agent assignments (Atlas=SEO, Ellie=Content, Vesper=Social, Rowan=Analytics, Aria=Research)
- Running notes section

## New Client Onboarding Steps (Solomon SOP)

1. **Create project** from template: `basecamp templates construct 46682962 --name "[CLIENT] - Slacked Services"`
2. **Customize docs**: Replace all `[CLIENT NAME]` placeholders
3. **Fill Strategy Notes**: Service tier, budget, KPIs, content pillars
4. **Set due dates**: Add dates to Month 1 deliverables
5. **Invite client**: Add client contact to project (they'll only see visible items)
6. **Assign agents**: Update Internal Work Queue with specific agent tasks
7. **Kick off**: Client receives welcome message notification automatically
